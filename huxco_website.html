Who We Are

HuxCo is a forward-facing investment holding company operating at the intersection of technological transformation and financial innovation. We identify, evaluate, and support ventures that define the next epoch of human capability and economic infrastructure.

What We Invest In

Our investment mandate centers on three critical domains: decentralized financial systems that reshape value exchange, artificial intelligence infrastructure that augments human intelligence, and deep technology solutions that solve fundamental constraints in computation, materials, and autonomous systems.

Where We Operate

We maintain a global perspective with strategic focus on emerging technology hubs across North America, Europe, and Asia. Our network encompasses institutional partners, banking counterparties, and regulatory frameworks that enable compliant capital deployment at scale.

Investment Thesis

Our strategic framework for identifying and evaluating transformational investment opportunities.

Crypto

Decentralization & Digital Value

We invest in protocols, infrastructure, and applications that fundamentally restructure how value is created, stored, and transferred. Our focus encompasses layer-1 blockchain protocols, decentralized finance primitives, and institutional-grade custody solutions that enable the transition to programmable money.

Artificial Intelligence

Intelligence Infrastructure

Our AI investment thesis centers on foundational models, compute infrastructure, and specialized applications that augment human decision-making capabilities. We target ventures building the computational substrate for artificial general intelligence and domain-specific AI systems with clear economic utility.

Deep Technology

Materials, Computation & Autonomy

We identify breakthrough technologies that solve fundamental physical and computational constraints. Our portfolio includes advanced materials science, quantum computing systems, autonomous robotics, and biotechnology platforms that enable new categories of human capability and economic productivity.
